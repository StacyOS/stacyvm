---

## Brand Principles

- Elegant serif typography for hierarchy and personality.
- Clean sans-serif typography for readability.
- Monospaced labels for utility and metadata.
- Warm neutral palette with vibrant accent color.
- Rounded components to create a friendly, modern interface.
